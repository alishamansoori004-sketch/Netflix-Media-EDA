Key Findings
Movies dominate Netflix's content library.
Most content was released and added after 2015.
The United States is the leading content producer.
Drama and International Movies are the most common genres.
Most content is rated TV-MA and TV-14.
Standard movie duration ranges between 80–120 minutes.


Conclusion
This project demonstrates practical skills in data cleaning, visualization, and business insight generation. The findings help understand Netflix's content strategy and showcase the application of Python-based data analysis.
